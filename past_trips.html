Past Mission Trips

North Dakota — 2023

Our team served at a local outreach that focused on equipping youth leaders and strengthening the local church. It was a time of growth and hands-on ministry experience that shaped our heart for community discipleship.

Montana — 2024

This trip centered around building connections and learning from missionaries who have served in long-term rural ministry. It gave us an inside look at what day-to-day mission life looks like in the field.

Arizona — 2022

Before heading into our longer assignments, we joined a short-term mission focused on serving Native communities. It was an incredible opportunity to listen, learn, and grow in cross-cultural ministry.
